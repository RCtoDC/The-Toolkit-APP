**Media Production 100 GmbH**

**Expertise:** Digitization workflows, 3D & XR production

**Role in RCtoDC**  
Media Production 100 GmbH is a leading partner in the RCtoDC project and responsible for the development of the Toolkit. The organization contributes extensive practical experience in 3D digitization, scanning, photogrammetry, XR workflows, and production pipelines.

**Key Areas of Advice**

- Planning and executing digitization workflows
- Equipment selection and setup
- Quality control of 3D assets
- Sustainable reuse of 3D data

**Expert Tip**

_Focus on consistent workflows and documentation from the first scan to the final output to ensure long-term reuse._

**Orsula d.o.o.**

**Expertise:** Project management, standards, cultural heritage digitization

**Role in RCtoDC**  
Orsula contributes expertise in cultural heritage digitization strategies, project coordination, and alignment with international standards. Their guidance ensures methodological consistency and interoperability across the Toolkit.

**Key Areas of Advice**

- Digitization planning and coordination
- Standards integration
- Documentation methodologies
- Institutional workflows

**Expert Tip**

_Digitization is not only a technical task but a strategic process that must align with institutional goals and standards._
